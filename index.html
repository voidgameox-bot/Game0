About Lights Out

How to play

The board starts with some tiles lit. Click any tile to toggle it and its orthogonal neighbors (up, down, left, right). Your goal is to turn all tiles off.

  • Choose grid size and difficulty, then start a New Game.
  • Timer starts on your first move. Reset returns to the starting state.
  • Clear the board to win!
